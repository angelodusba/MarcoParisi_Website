ABOUT ME

Marco is an Italian filmmaker with a deep passion for evocative imagery and storytelling. He began his career in Italy as a photographer and cameraman in the concert scene while studying at the Italian Academy of Visual Communication (ILAS) in Naples. After completing his studies, he gained hands-on experience working for leading Italian television studios, including Mediaset, Rai, and Discovery Plus in Rome, contributing to television broadcasting programmes. Since 2022, Marco has pursued an international freelance career, specialising in storytelling, directing, and video content for social media and other digital platforms. His work is strategically aligned with brand identity and audience engagement, ensuring that each project not only tells a compelling story but also reinforces the brand’s vision and market positioning. In his projects, he oversees the entire video production process, from concept development and direction to editing and final delivery, while also coordinating key roles within the production. Marco’s approach to storytelling is dynamic and innovative, with a strong photographic eye that brings each project to life. He constantly explores new techniques, refining his style and pushing creative boundaries. For Marco, artistic exploration is essential. He continuously develops his skills and embraces new challenges, ensuring every project is thoughtfully crafted with a unique narrative that captures his client’s vision.

About me

Marco is an Italian filmmaker whose passion for evocative imagery and visual storytelling knows no bounds. He began his career in Italy as a photographer and cameraman in the concert scene, honing his skills at the Italian Academy of Visual Communication ILAS in Naples while actively working in the field. After completing his studies, he continued his training by collaborating with Italy’s leading television studios, including Mediaset, Rai, and Discovery Plus in Rome. Driven by the desire to expand his creative and professional horizons, Marco decided to move abroad, where he found new inspiration and growth opportunities. Today, he works between Italy, Belgium, and London, creating brand films, commercials, fashion content, corporate videos, event coverage, and content for leading social platforms. His approach to storytelling is fresh and innovative, while his photographic skills bring each project to life through unique and engaging visuals. With boundless imagination and an unwavering work ethic, Marco constantly pushes himself beyond his limits, exploring new narrative and visual techniques. For Marco, artistic experimentation is essential and forms the foundation of every project. He is always striving to expand his skills and training, consistently working towards improvement. Each project is custom-made, with a personalised narrative that reflects the essence of the client.
